Know Why You Believe
Paul Little

Date Finished: July 2022


This book is written in a 12 part Q&A. Each question is one that Paul Little, the author, has heard from colleges numerous times. I liked this point, because I can use this to back up my claims for Christianity and clearly define some for non-Christians I meet. Some of these questions even I ask a lot. Contrasted to CS Lewis, in his answers, Little uses more scripture and scientific works to back up his claims. This book was more apologetic than Mere Christianity, because it serves a different purpose. Mere Christianity is a look at the need for Christ and morality through philosophical reasoning; Little uses scripture and scientific evidence to back-up claims. As someone who wants to memorize and know more scripture in general, these claim-backing verses are super useful. He adds to CS Lewis arguments at times, such as when he counters the ‘Legend’ argument to the Trilemma.
