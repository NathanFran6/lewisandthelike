Know Why You Believe
Paul Little

Date Finished: July 2022


This book is written in a 12 part Q&A. Each question is one that Paul Little, the author, has heard from colleges numerous times. I liked this point, because I can use this to back up my claims for Christianity and clearly define some for non-Christians I meet. Some of these questions even I ask a lot. Contrasted to CS Lewis, in his answers, Little uses more scripture and scientific works to back up his claims. This book was more apologetic than Mere Christianity, because it serves a different purpose. Mere Christianity is a look at the need for Christ and morality through philosophical reasoning; Little uses scripture and scientific evidence to back-up claims. As someone who wants to memorize and know more scripture in general, these claim-backing verses are super useful. He adds to CS Lewis arguments at times, such as when he counters the ‘Legend’ argument to the Trilemma. He delves into a little scientific work too, which is intertesting as he used a lot of other scientific work to back up claims and gave his views on a few topics without stating his overall view on the whole argument. For example, he leaves the question of whether the big bang happened or the age of the earth open, for us to decide if there is enough evidence for either side. Of course, he states the clear importance of Jesus and our salvation, not to be overshaadowed by third tier arguments. Overall, this book is a guide to some tough intellectual questions about Christinaity which can help me and the unbeliever alike.
