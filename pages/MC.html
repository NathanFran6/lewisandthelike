Mere Christianity
C.S. Lewis

Date Finished: July 2022


Even though I have been raised Christian and taught many doctrines he teaches in this book, I think Lewis puts all of these into poetic but concise language that anyone can really grasp. I found myself putting quotes down from the book every other page because of how memorable and thoughtful his words are. I would say my favorite section is the Christian Life. The first chapters are more of a proof of Christianity and why it is needed. This is needed for any doubters, of course, but the later chapters applied to Christian life and how we should live out our everyday.
